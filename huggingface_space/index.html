Pipeline overview: WARN Act notices (ground truth) fused with market data (yfinance), fundamentals (Alpha Vantage/FMP), news/social sentiment, and BLS macro indicators, aligned into a daily feature matrix via dbt + DuckDB (ASOF joins) and served through an XGBoost classifier with MLflow tracking and Airflow-orchestrated drift monitoring. Full architecture and code: github.com/hongming111/layoff-risk-prediction-pipeline
